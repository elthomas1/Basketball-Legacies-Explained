About

Basketball Legacies Explained is dedicated to showcasing the players that have helped to build the sport of basketabll to be what it is today.We do this by breaking the age barrier and bringing awareness to the legendary careers of players from ALL eras (1945-present day).Here you will find stat representation, detailed scouting reports, summaries of who each of these player were and how they impacted the game. You can also find links to sites, and videos where you can access more information on these players careers.
